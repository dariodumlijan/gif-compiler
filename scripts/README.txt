Step by step how to install guide

1. Open "preinstall", ONLY if you didn't have any previous version installed
    1. Type in your computer password, no characters will be shown (this is normal)
    2. Press enter and wait for the script to end (it may take a while)
2. Move the "Gif Compiler" to "Applications"

If you encounter the "Unidentified developer"/"App cannot be opened" error
1. Open "System Preferences#
2. Go to "Security & Privacy" and select the "General" tab
3. Click "Open Anyway"

Done! Hope it helps :)
